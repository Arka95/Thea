# Sequential Problem Solver

Invoke by saying: "think step by step about X" or "break this down"

---

Use the sequential_thinking MCP tool to solve this problem methodically.

## Process:

1. **Restate** the problem in your own words (verify understanding)
2. **Identify constraints** — what must be true, what can't change
3. **List approaches** — at least 2 different ways to solve this
4. **Evaluate tradeoffs** — pros/cons of each approach
5. **Choose** the best approach with justification
6. **Break into steps** — ordered list of atomic actions
7. **Identify risks** — what could go wrong at each step
8. **Proceed** with implementation

Use this for: architecture decisions, debugging complex issues, optimization problems, refactoring plans.
